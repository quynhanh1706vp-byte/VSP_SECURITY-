Frequently asked questions.
What's included in the free trial? +
The 30-day free trial includes full Professional plan access — AI Advisor, RASP protection, SBOM tracking, and all compliance features. No credit card required to start. Your scan data and P4 baseline carry over when you subscribe.
How long does it take to achieve P4? +
Most organizations achieve P4 in 4–6 months with VSP. The timeline depends on your starting compliance posture, asset count, and how quickly POA&M items are remediated. VSP's AI Advisor reduces remediation time by 60% on average.
Do you support air-gapped / classified environments? +
Yes — the Enterprise plan includes on-premise deployment packages for air-gapped environments. VSP has been tested at IL4/IL5 classification levels. Contact our sales team for a tailored deployment plan.
What 3PAOs do you work with? +
VSP has established relationships with Coalfire, A-LIGN, Schellman, and Tevora. Our OSCAL exports and audit artifacts are accepted directly by all FedRAMP-authorized 3PAOs, reducing assessment preparation time significantly.
Is billing monthly or annual? +
Both options are available. Annual billing saves 20% and includes a dedicated Customer Success Manager. Government purchase orders (POs) and agency credit cards are accepted. Net-30 invoicing available for Enterprise customers.
Can I migrate from Tenable or Qualys? +
Yes — VSP includes a migration assistant that imports existing scan data, POA&M spreadsheets, and SSP documents. Most customers are fully migrated within 2 weeks. We provide a dedicated migration engineer for Professional and Enterprise plans.
